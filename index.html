About me

Welcome to my page! I am a passionate explorer of the digital world, excited to discover innovation and technological possibilities. My mission is to combine creativity with technology to create unique experiences.

My main focus is aviation technology and drones, which I consider tools that open up new perspectives and possibilities for us. With more than five years of experience in the field of drones, I specialized in creating aerial shots and innovative projects.

With my interest in aviation technology and passion for drones, I was constantly learning and pushing my limits. I have acquired certifications and skills in drone piloting to provide safe and professional aerial footage. My deep knowledge of the technical aspects of drones allows me to solve any challenges and achieve maximum quality in every project.

My skills don't stop at flying itself, but also in post-production, where I can transform raw material into a final form that appeals and inspires.
